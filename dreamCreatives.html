Dream Creatives Redesign

Overview

Dream Creatives is an event product website that utilizes AI to transform its guest’s images into immersive, themed keepsakes. This project was done as part of the FLUI 2026 Design Jam hosted at Emily Carr University, where my team and I worked together to redesign the clients existing website over a weekend. Given the strict timeline, we worked on evaluating the current experience and designing a refined site that better represented our client’s goals.

Role

Co-Lead UI/UX Designer (1 of 2 designers, team of 4)

Tools

Figma, Notion, Google Workspace
